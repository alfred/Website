A Little About Myself

If you're reading this, I bet you probably want a little more of an explanation. Here goes! I was born and raised in Worcester, MA. I started spending all my time on computers in 2004 when I started playing computer games. Within months, I found myself spending all my free time online, playing games and learning cool new things. Thus, begins the most condensed story about me ever told.

In an effort to keep our archaic computer functioning and to continue satisfying my gaming habits, I had to make many tweaks to my configuration. I managed to learn a lot about troubleshooting computers by repeatedly breaking my operating system and having to fix it before my parents came home from work that day. Needless to say, I was not always 100% successful, but I have always learned from my failures!

When I'm not tinkering with my computer I enjoy reading comics. I am a few volumes short of a full encyclopedia of the DC Universe. The complex web of relationships between all the heroes, anti-heroes, and villains amazes me. Many characters have been around for over half a century but still find their way into new dangerous and exciting situations. It's always exciting to see what trouble your favorite hero is going to get into next.

Comics are just one of my hobbies. I listen to music almost constantly. The way music can control our mood makes it a powerful force. My favorite artists are Chance The Rapper, Ab-Soul, Chidlish Gambino, Dizzy Wright, and Jhen√© Aiko and a few more (I'm trying to avoid name dropping here). I also took up longboarding recently. I picked it up fast and am now working on learning to longboard dance. I even took the opportunity to design my own longboard decal. I think that trying new things is the key to becoming a well-rounded person, so I always make an attempt to say yes to new opportunities.
